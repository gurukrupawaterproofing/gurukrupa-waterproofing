About Gurukrupa Waterproofing

Gurukrupa Waterproofing is a trusted waterproofing company based in Pune, providing reliable and long-lasting waterproofing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

Our experienced team specializes in Bathroom Waterproofing, Terrace Waterproofing, Balcony Waterproofing, Wall Crack Repair, Epoxy Tile Grouting, Thermal Leak Detection, and Chemical Waterproofing using premium quality materials.

Our mission is to protect your property from leakage, seepage, dampness, and water damage with advanced waterproofing technology and professional workmanship.

Technology Used for Leak Detection & Repair

We use advanced leak detection technology to identify hidden water leakage accurately without unnecessary damage.

Thermal Leak Detector

Our Thermal Leak Detector uses infrared imaging technology to identify hidden moisture, seepage, and water leaks behind walls, floors, ceilings, terraces, and bathrooms without damaging the structure.

Endoscopic Inspection Camera

Our Endoscopic Inspection Camera allows us to inspect concealed pipelines, wall cavities, drainage systems, and hard-to-reach areas to locate the exact source of leakage with precision.
